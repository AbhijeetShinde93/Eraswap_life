What is Era Swap Life Wallet?

ES Life Wallet is a free, open-source, client-side interface. ES Life Wallet allows you to interact directly with the blockchain, while you remain in full control of your keys and funds.

Please think about this carefully. YOU are the one who is in control. ES Life Wallet is not a bank or exchange. We don't hold your keys, your funds, or your information. This means we can't access accounts, recover keys, reset passwords, or reverse transactions.
